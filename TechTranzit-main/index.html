About Us

In the modern era where there are thousands of blooming startups being established every day we bring to you our small family "TechtranZit". Under the supervision of the Institute of engineering technology, Dr. Shakuntala Misra National Rehabilitation University and with the guidence of Dr. C.K. Dixit we present to you a platform to enhance and define your expertise for a better tomorrow.

We at Techtranzit aim at working towards an enhanced working space within our campus and to introduce our peers to the advanced modern world which they Gonna embark in the near future as promising young talents working towards promising a future full of opportunities for them as well as for their counterpart.
